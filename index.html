About

Our story ...

  • 2011

    An idea was born

    Being connected & digitally active was no longer a luxury but also a necessity, and accessing consumers online was vital to any business' growth & success. Accordingly, Fanbase was created to build & manage an effective relationship between organizations & their client base online.

  • 2011-2013

    Our early days

    With the main focus on the ever growing social media platforms, our main service was to develop state of the art strategy for organizations that is in line with their business goals to properly communicate & connect with their fan base. We started providing a comprehensive approach that aims to holistically identify the most effective strategy that is best needed to match a company's digital goals & vision, aid in the development of the online brand identity, and moderate online presence to attain maximum engagement to ensure the creation of a sustainable relationship between companies & their consumers.

  • 2014

    Expansion

    With the successful transition from a startup to a micro company, 2014 was the year of expansion for Fanbase; we relocated into bigger offices in a more commercial neighborhood in Amman as well as almost doubling the size of the team to be able to serve our client base that came from Jordan, the Levant & the GCC countries. Moreover, our services & offerings grew from social media limited to providing holistic digital marketing & advertising services to our wide & diverse portfolio of clients.

  • Today

    Where we are?

    Fanbase is a regional boutique digital advertising agency made up of a team of dedicated young professionals that specializes in helping organizations gain maximum exposure for their brands, have a wider reach towards their target audience, and build a sustainable relationship between the company's brand image & their customers.
